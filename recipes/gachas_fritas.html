Description

This is a very simple recipe, my mother used to say to me it was eaten after the spanish civil war, where ingredients where poor, therefore even if you think you have nothing in the fridge you can still do this recipe. This recipe has a special place in my heart because my mother used to make it for me when I was little.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Garlic
  • Ñora
  • Olive oil

The original recipe my mother tought me has Garlic and Ñora as main ingredients (Ñora is an spanish pepper), but you can put whatever you like in the dough. I recommend you ham and chorizo as a second option.
